Requirements:

A laptop/PC on Windows 10/Windows 7 (Might work on other versions of Windows. Can be done on a mac, but the process differs)

An iPhone or IPad device

AltStore (Can be downloaded here: https://altstore.io/)

iCloud (AltStore won't work without it. Get it here: https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204283 )

AltServerPatcher (Needed for installing your own IPAs. Can be found here: https://ipg.pw/altstore/AltServerPatcher.exe)

Web server for Chrome (To put your IPAs on a local webserver. Its an app for Chrome, here is the link: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/web-server-for-chrome/ofhbbkphhbklhfoeikjpcbhemlocgigb)

The IPA you want to install. For this tutorial I'll use uncover from this link: https://github.com/pwn20wndstuff/Undecimus/releases)

Steps:

Once you have everything downloaded and installed, open the turn on the webserver (Which should be in the Apps section of chrome).
Choose the folder which has your IPA. (Should be in the Downloads folder).

Once after its done and the server is turned on, go to 127.0.0.1:8887 and you should see the IPA listed in there.

Locate the IPA in there, right-click it and copy link address.

Now open AltServerPatcher (In administrator mode) and on the dropdown make sure its "Custom IPA Link" and paste the address which you copied in Step 4 onto the IPA URL and patch it. (If it cannot fit inside there, rename the IPA to something with little characters and repeat from Step 3)

After its done patching, open AltStore and connect your IOS device to your PC.

There will be a an icon in your notification panel, Right-click that and install AltStore.

Enter your AppleID details which will be used for the installing. (If it gives an error -22400, try again but this time do not use caps on your AppleID. Example: PeterPan@apple.com -> peterpan@apple.com).

It will now install it on your device and you will be notified when its successful.

On your iPhone/IPad go to Settings>General>Profile & Device Management and trust the Developer App

open the app and enjoy it.
