Jailbreak







If this is your first time jailbreaking, please do some research about your device and iOS version, to check if it's even possible for you to jailbreak right now. If your iOS/iDevice supports jailbreaking that requires an app install, you're probably in the right place. If you go to the 'Jailbreak' portion of our apps section, you'll see a list of downloadable IPAs for jailbreaking. Let's say you need to install the odyssey jailbreak, well first of all you'll probably need AltStore. As long as you have a computer, you can do that easily. Following the above altstore tutorial, sideload the jailbreak ipa into altstore, then restart your device if it doesn't show up on the homescreen. After that, enter the app and follow the steps to jailbreak, which usually just involves pressing a big large button labelled 'Jailbreak'!
