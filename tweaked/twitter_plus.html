Twitter++

Description:
Twitter++ is a tweaked modification to the iOS social-media app Twitter.

Features:
 - Share images and links from tweets
 - Share tweets to third-party apps
 - Save video and GIFs to your device
 - Download YouTube videos from Twitter
 - Set the default browser to open links
 - Show the keyboard on Compose instead of media screen
 - Automatic Long Tweet Creation
 - Displays a confirmation when favoriting a tweet
 - Enable full-screen view
 - Video tweets of users who have blocked you
