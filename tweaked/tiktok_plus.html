TikTok++

Description:
TikTok++ is a tweaked modification to the iOS social-media and video sharing app TikTok.

Features:
 - Download Videos
 - Following Page enhancements
 - Hide all buttons and text views for videos
 - Comment more than 90 characters for videos
 - Present the download button in For You page
 - Perform all share actions
 - Download User's Profile Image
 - Show date created for videos
 - View Private or Under Review video comments
 - Spoof Following, Fan and Hearts stats
 - Display User's information and video count
 - Sort videos by likes in descending order
 - Upload videos up to 30min. length
 - Join live streams 24/7
 - Removed TikTok Ads
